FAQ

Why does angular-primer require angularjs 1.3 or higher?

See https://github.com/angular/angular.js/issues/7383

Does angular-primer only visualize primers?

No, while originally designed for the visualization of DNA/RNA primers, the angular-primer directives composable elements can be used together to create a simple, or complex, SVG visualizations of any strand based genomic features.

Who developed angular-primer?

angular-primer was developed by Jayson Harshbarger at RIKEN Yokohama institute. This work was supported by a research grant from the Japanese Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology (MEXT) to the RIKEN Center for Life Science Technologies.
